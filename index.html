FASEP Kaduna Deployment Plan
About the project

As part of the FASEP project in Kaduna City, a smart and inclusive street lighting project, Mitsio Motu conducted a study to determine the locations for the deployment of 100 Sunna Design solar streetlights (25 ISSL+, 50 Maxi road, 25 Maxi 4).

Throughout the project, several deployment plans and methodologies have been set up to identify, categorize the most impactful areas & streets for the deployment of streetlights.

Mitsio Motu team mapped every infrastructure, every streetlight and electricity line in Barnawa Area. As a result, 34 types of assets were identified and everyone of them was mapped and accessible on an interactive map.

Based on this data driven study, a deployment plan has been finalized and been validated by KAPSCO.

Innovation

This project brought an opportunity to test and create models for large scale vision of deployment based on accurate data collected on the field.

After the data collection of Barnawa Area and after the deployment plan, Mitsio Motu conducted a data collection campaign and mapped every streetlight of Kaduna city.

Based on this mapping , Mitsio Motu created an analysis to quantify the needs of streetlights on the whole city. This plan allows to identify all areas who do not have access to public lighting and therefore plan and budget deployment plans to grant equal access to streetlight at the city level.

This data platform displays all of these collected pieces of information to offer access to the mapping of the city for decision makers of the Kaduna State.
